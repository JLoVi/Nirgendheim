Hi to all 3D total members and fans !

Once again i'm here to make a short tutorial about the new outstandiang 3D total CD nº11 Texture - Alien Organic.This new CD is full of textures about aliens organic,they are amazing, great combinations of skins and surfaces, a unique collection that give us the chance to create great enviroments and strange characters, organic forms, and everything that you could possible imagine. My intend was create some organic form based on a kind of mix between an alien and an octopus. When i start the modelling part, i had the form in my mind, but it was more difficult that i espect, because there's no references, i had to create some unusual, very organic and in a sort of way a creature from another world.

 

Main Scene - Modelling

I decide to use ZBrush2, this powerful software are the perfect tool to give a great organic look to my model. I start with Z-spheres, this one of the great tools from zbrush, easy and intuitive, after that with an adaptative skin tool, give some subdivision to achieve a good resolutions to my mesh, very important to the next step: sculpting the model, here the 3 steps of the creations method of my alien organic model.

I had in my mind this scenario, some unknown creature was capture and unidentified, possible danger. The next challenge was insert the model in an enviroment, but the main purpose was to give a great look to the model, so the texture was a very important part. I dicide to compose a scene where the creature is hanging by strong cables and a kind of machine with claws. The cables were made with a several cones and path deform. The machine with claws are poly modelling and the hair part made with scatter method. Here a wire of the final scene.

 

Main Scene - Texturing

Well, and here came the challeging part, the cd have a great variety of skins, and i dicide to make a simple mix between two texture with a dirt mask. Making some test with other texture these two were the best for my alien skin, i just add some fallof shaders just to turn the texture more organic looking. Because of the complex of the model i had to uwnrap them to fix the tile. I try to make the skin a lit bit viscous by adding reflection.

The cables had some dirt and reflection the same material of the machine with claws, the fence is a texture with a alpha opacity and a plate a metal mix material.

I made also some skin test for my alien organic model, the variety of textures are great, and we can achieve some good looking skin textures, with plenty combinations, it's very intresting this kind of exercise, because the results are quite surprising, and a few adjustments or just changing a mask map can make the difference.

 

Main Scene - Lights

The scene was rendering with Vray, i use a single but powerful burn yellow light to give a dramatic look to the scene. Here the light study that i made after the texturization step.After the rendering i made some post production in photoshop, some levels and contrast adjustments, sharp and other final touches.

 

Final notes
