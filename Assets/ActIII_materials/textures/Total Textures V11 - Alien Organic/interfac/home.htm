Welcome to Total Textures Vol 11

Total Textures v11 is a collection of Alienesque organic textures. From the weird and slimey, to more subtly toned skins, these textures are made up from many layers and elements captured by 50% photography and 50% hand painted.

Some of the textures also have Specular maps included. If a texture has the specular option available it appears as the following icon in between the diffuse/colour map and the bump/alpha map in each of the sections. Right click this icon to save the .jpg file to your texture directory.

The CD Includes:

  • 117 Colour texture maps.
  • 117 Bump or Alpha maps. (with this collection, endless combinations of Colour Maps and bump maps)
  • 36 Specular Maps

    Split up into the following categories:
  • 9 Blues
  • 25 Browns
  • 22 Greens
  • 16 Greys
  • 14 Reds
  • 12 Yellows
  • 19 Miscellaneous

  • Html interface allowing you to browse through all of the contents on the CD and save your chosen maps to a directory of your choice. Use the links on the left hand side to browse the various sections and click the image thumbnails to access the maps.
